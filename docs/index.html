THE FOUR DOTS

One platform. Four superpowers.

Each dot is a capability. Together, they form a nexus — and at the center is the single dot that matters most: your thought.

01

Agentic Workflow

Give it a goal. Watch it plan, pick tools, take action, and come back with the answer. Calculators, code, search, your custom tools — all orchestrated autonomously.

  • Tool-choosing intelligence
  • Plug-in any custom action
  • Transparent reasoning trail
02

RAG System

Drop in your docs, slides, PDFs — Nexus reads them, remembers them, and answers from them. Your data becomes your copilot's long-term memory.

  • Drag-and-drop knowledge
  • Context-aware retrieval
  • Private, local vector storage
03

MCP Server

Connect external services — databases, APIs, enterprise systems — via the open Model Context Protocol. Bring the outside world into the conversation.

  • Open standard, any provider
  • Toggle servers per chat
  • Composable, not locked-in
04

Multi-LLM

Claude, GPT, Llama, Mistral — switch models in a click. Use the best brain for the task, or keep everything local with Ollama. Zero vendor lock-in.

  • Anthropic, OpenAI, Ollama
  • Swap mid-conversation
  • Cloud or fully offline
